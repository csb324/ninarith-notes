# Victor's Gloves

[Owned by::[[Costi]]]
[Origin::[[Victor]]]
[Attunement:: yes]

Item, Simple brown leather gloves that seem to be extraordinarily nimble.

Grants the wearer +5 to sleight of hand checks, lockpicking, and contested dexterity attempts to counterspell.
